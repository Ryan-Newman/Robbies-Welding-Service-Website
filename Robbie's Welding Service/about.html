Type of Weld How it works
MIG A process of welding that uses a wire that is fed out of the rod that melts, when cooled the weld is complete.
TIG Two handed welding process, where one hand holds the torch and the other hand add in a filler metal.
Stick Stick Welding uses welding rods to create the weld by using Direct Current to melt and merge the metal to the object.
